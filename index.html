• Doreamon

  • Shinchan

  • Ben 10

    Ben Tennyson finds a mysterious watch (Omnitrix) that turns him into powerful aliens. He uses it to fight villains and save the day.

  • Kick Buttowski

    A fearless suburban kid dreams of becoming the world's greatest daredevil. Kick performs wild stunts with his loyal friend Gunther.

  • Billy and Mandy

    Two kids befriend the Grim Reaper after winning a bet. Dark humor and weird creatures make this show uniquely entertaining.

  • Chota Bheem

  • DragonballZ

    Goku and his friends defend Earth from powerful foes through intense battles and Super Saiyan transformations. A legendary anime filled with action and courage.

  • Horrid Henry

    A mischievous boy constantly gets into trouble, often clashing with his goody-goody brother Perfect Peter. Full of pranks and rebellion.

  • Johnny Test

    An adventurous boy with genius scientist sisters goes on wild experiments. His loyal dog Dukey joins him in every chaotic mission.

  • Cars 95

    A fast and flashy racecar learns life lessons in the town of Radiator Springs. His journey shows friendship, humility, and growth.

  • Mr Bean

    A quirky man with childlike behavior ends up in comical situations. Minimal dialogue, but maximum laughs through his expressions and actions.

  • Phineas and Ferb

    Stepbrothers create amazing inventions during their summer vacation. Meanwhile, their pet Perry secretly fights evil as Agent P.

  • Pink Panther

    A silent, clever pink-colored panther gets into amusing situations. Known for its slapstick comedy and iconic theme music.

  • Pokemon

    Ash Ketchum travels the world to become a Pokémon Master with his buddy Pikachu. Along the way, he battles, catches new Pokémon, and makes friends.

  • Power Rangers

    Teenagers with attitude morph into powerful rangers to fight evil forces and villians. Known for action-packed battles and teamwork.

  • Roll no. 21

  • Stuart Little

    A talking mouse adopted by a human family proves that size doesn’t matter. He embarks on heartwarming and brave adventures.

  • Tom and Jerry

    Classic cat-and-mouse rivalry full of nonstop chases, pranks, and laughs. Their battles are timeless and loved by all ages.

  • We bear bears

    Three bear brothers—Grizz, Panda, and Ice Bear—try to fit into human society. Their adventures are cute, funny, and often relatable.

  • Oggy and the Cockroaches

    Oggy is a lazy, loveable cat whose peaceful life is constantly disrupted by three mischievous cockroaches. With almost no dialogue, the show delivers hilarious comedy through wild chases and chaotic pranks.
